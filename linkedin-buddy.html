LinkedIn Buddy

Getting Started

Holy smokes, you're here!

Here's how to start using LinkedIn Buddy:

  1. Drag this link to your bookmarks bar: LinkedIn Buddy
  2. Open a LinkedIn tab and then click your new "LinkedIn Buddy" bookmark
  3. Click "Manage Templates" and set up your templates
  4. Navigate to a prospect's profile page and click the button in the bottom bar for template that you want to send

Tips

  1. Use the macros $FIRST_NAME and $COMPANY_NAME in your templates to automatically fill in the first name and company of your prospects
  2. Withdraw old connection requests periodically here
  3. Don't send more than ~1000 connection requests in a week
