Reset Gmeet Mic

What is this?

Reset Gmeet Mic is a bookmarklet (aka a script that you can save as a bookmark) that lets you instantly set your computer's preferred mic in Google Meet to the built-in microphone so that the output from your own bluetooth earbuds sounds better.

How do I use it?

  1. Drag this link to your bookmarks bar: Reset Gmeet Mic
  2. Whenever you join a Google Meet call, just click your new "Reset Gmeet Mic" bookmark
  3. Enjoy your calls with the best sound quality from your AirPods
